The
Titanic Dataset
Analytics

The Titanic was a luxury passenger ship that set sail from Southampton, England on April 10, 1912. It was on its way to New York City when it hit an iceberg on the evening of April 14. Despite the crew's attempts to keep the ship afloat, it eventually sank early in the morning on April 15. Many passengers and crew members were unable to get to the lifeboats in time and lost their lives in the cold water of the North Atlantic. The tragedy shocked the world and led to changes in maritime laws to ensure the safety of passengers at sea.

This analytics report presents information on the 891 passengers on the Titanic at the time of its sinking. The data includes various details about these individuals, such as their age, gender, and socio-economic status.
